# sales data analysis
This is my first git repository.
<br>
Conducted a comprehensive sales data analysis to support business decision-making using SQL. The project focused on evaluating sales performance across regions, sales representatives, and product categories to identify key drivers of revenue and profitability. Performed data cleaning, transformation, and analysis by creating structured databases and writing optimized SQL queries.

Analyzed critical business metrics such as total sales, profit margins, discount impact, and customer transaction behavior. Identified patterns showing that excessive discounting negatively affects profitability and that high sales do not always translate into high profit. Evaluated regional and product category performance to highlight high-performing areas and underperforming segments.

Generated actionable insights and recommendations, including optimizing discount strategies, focusing on high-margin products, and improving sales representative performance through better training and strategy alignment. This project demonstrates strong skills in SQL, data analysis, business problem-solving, and translating data into meaningful insights for strategic decision-making.
